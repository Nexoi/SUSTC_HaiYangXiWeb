Recent Advances in Nonlinear PDEs

2016-08-09 10:45:23
Objectives:
The aim of this workshop is to bring together researchers, and postgraduate students to present and discuss recent work on nonlinear partial differential equations (PDEs). The workshop will provide an opportunity for senior undergraduate students and postgraduate students to become familiar with the current state of the art in this important area of mathematics.
The morning sessions of the workshop will be dedicated to invited and contributed talks, and the afternoons will be used for informal discussions and collaborations.
⁃ Before the workshop, Professor Fanghua Lin of the Courant Institute of NYU will give a mini-course from December 16 to 19, 2016. Professor Lin's talk will discuss basic elliptic PDEs to De Georgi-Nash-Moser and Krylov-Safanov theorems. This mini-course is accessible to senior undergraduate students who have taken a basic course in PDEs.
